Company Info

Triangle Coatings, your total coatings solution.

Triangle Coatings’ business began in 1932 as a small, regional paint manufacturer.

We offered users quality coatings, quick service, and immediate access to chemical information and technical advice. As we grew, we established ourselves as a reliable and knowledgeable source for standard and specialty paints and coatings made available in small and large quantities.

Today, through expanded manufacturing facilities and modern laboratories, Triangle Coatings, Inc. offers a full line of Architectural & Decorative Paints, Oil Paints, Concrete Sealers, Industrial Maintenance Coatings, OEM Product Finishes, and Government Specification Coatings. We also offer custom formulation and toll manufacturing for customers with specific demands.

An environmentally conscientious company, Triangle Coatings is responsible for developing environmentally friendly coatings, including High Reflective and Smart Coatings, which help increase the energy efficiency of buildings and other manufacturing and construction projects.

You’ll find our coatings on NASA, NATO and National Radio Observatory large antennas; bridges, including the Golden Gate Bridge; refineries, including Chevron Refineries; utility equipment for PG&E; and thousands of other surfaces for customers around the world.

Our broad reaching and valued clientele is due in large part to responsive and friendly customer service. Working together in a relaxed family atmosphere, our staff goes the extra mile to manufacture and ship only the finest quality paints and coatings.
