Privacy Policy

Effective date: to be confirmed by Triangle Coatings. Legal review required

This policy describes how this website currently behaves. Some statements are placeholders that must be reviewed and confirmed by Triangle Coatings and, where appropriate, legal counsel before publication. Items needing confirmation are marked Legal review required.

Who we are

This website is operated by Triangle Coatings Inc., 5625 Brisa Street, Unit A, Livermore, CA 94550. Phone: 1-925-583-0800. Email: sales@tricoat.com.

Information you submit through forms

When you use the recommendation, quote, order-request, or order-status forms, your browser opens your own email application addressed to a Triangle Coatings inbox (for example sales@tricoat.com or orders@tricoat.com). The website itself does not store this information on a server. The details you send — such as name, email, phone, project details, and any shipping address you provide — are received by email and used only to respond to your request.

Accounts

This website does not offer online customer accounts and does not store login credentials. Any account or discount handling occurs directly with our team.

Order requests and payment

Order requests submitted through this website are requests for pricing and availability, not completed purchases. No payment is collected or processed on this website, and no payment-card information is requested online. Legal review required if a payment processor is added in the future.

Cookies and local storage

This website does not set advertising or analytics cookies. It uses your browser's local storage on your own device to remember the items in your request list between pages. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us. Clearing your browser storage removes it.

Automatically collected technical information

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. As with most web hosting, the hosting provider may process standard technical information such as IP address and request logs to deliver the site and for security. Legal review required to confirm the hosting provider's current data practices.

Embedded content and third-party services

  • Web fonts: This site loads fonts from Google Fonts. Your browser requests those files from Google's servers, which may process your IP address. Legal review required
  • Social links: Links to social platforms are plain links; those platforms have their own privacy policies.

Service, shipping, and payment providers

If you place an order with us directly, shipping carriers and, where applicable, payment providers will receive the information needed to fulfill and process that order. Legal review required to list the specific providers used.

Data retention

Email you send us is retained as part of our business correspondence. A specific retention period has not been established here and Legal review required.

Data security

The website is served over HTTPS. We do not claim any specific encryption of stored business records unless and until confirmed. Legal review required

Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information you have sent us, or to opt out of certain processing. To make a request, contact sales@tricoat.com. Legal review required for California (CCPA/CPRA) and other jurisdiction-specific rights.
