Welcome to Triangle Coatings!

Your total coatings solution since 1932.

Since 1932, Triangle Coatings has provided customers with high quality paints and coatings, responsive service, and immediate access to chemical information and technical advice.

Today our expanded manufacturing facilities and modern laboratories support Architectural & Decorative Paints, Oil Paints, Concrete Sealers, Industrial Maintenance Coatings, OEM Product Finishes, Government Specification Coatings, custom formulation, and toll manufacturing.

We remain focused on quality, innovation, environmental responsibility, and friendly customer service for customers around the world.
