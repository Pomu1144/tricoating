Manufacturing Coatings Since 1932

More than 90 years of coatings expertise.

Since 1932, Triangle Coatings has supplied high-quality paints and coatings, responsive service, and direct access to technical advice. Today our manufacturing facilities and laboratories support architectural and decorative paints, oil paints, concrete sealers, industrial maintenance coatings, OEM finishes, government-specification coatings, custom formulation, and toll manufacturing.

  1. 1932Founded as a regional paint manufacturer.
  2. GrowthExpanded into industrial, OEM, and government-specification coatings.
  3. TodayCustom formulation and toll manufacturing for customers in the U.S. and internationally.
