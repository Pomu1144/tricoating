REFINISHING PROCESS

Surface Preparation & Professional Glazing

01

Surface Preparation

  • Deep industrial cleaning
  • Silicone & caulk removal
  • Acid etching
  • Sanding for adhesion
  • Repairing chips & cracks
  • Final solvent wipe
  • Masking & protection
  • Tack rag dust removal
02

Primer Application

The primer coating is professionally mixed and evenly sprayed onto the prepared surface to maximize adhesion and durability.

Primer Ratio

70% Primer / 30% Reducer

03

Final Glaze Finish

The final coating is chemically induced and professionally sprayed to produce a smooth, high-gloss, factory-like finish.

Final Coat Ratio

50% White Porcelain

30% Activator

20% Reducer
