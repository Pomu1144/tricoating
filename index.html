OUR PROCESS

Professional Surface Preparation & Glazing

01

Surface Preparation

  • Deep industrial cleaning
  • Silicone & caulk removal
  • Acid etching of porcelain & ceramic
  • Sanding for mechanical adhesion
  • Repairing chips & cracks
  • Final solvent wipe
  • Masking surrounding areas
  • Tack rag dust removal
02

Primer Coat Application

The professionally mixed primer system is sprayed evenly onto the prepared surface to create strong adhesion before glazing.

Primer Mix Ratio

70% Primer / 30% Reducer

03

Final Glaze Finish

The final coating is mixed, induced, and professionally sprayed to create a smooth, factory-like finish with long-term durability.

Final Coat Ratio

50% White Porcelain

30% Activator

20% Reducer
